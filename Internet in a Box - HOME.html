Insert a USB Stick/Drive to Publish Files Here

Teachers/Librarians: your class handouts (PDF files, photos, videos, etc) will instantly appear RIGHT HERE after you insert a USB stick/drive into the Internet-in-a-Box. Students can upload their work by clicking on the Upload to USB button.

Any teacher can optionally create a folder named PUBLIC on their USB stick, if student uploads need to be private.
